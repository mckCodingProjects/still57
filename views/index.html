Values

Our Design Principles

Gameplay

Gameplay is in the heart of our games.We test different gameplay mechanics to perfection and build around that, making sure our players have the best casual mobile gaming experience.

Content

There is nothing worse than a good game that feels empty. Every game should have rich content that players look forward to. We make sure to pour tons of content so our players feel fulfilled.

Freedom

Customization is something that really lacks in casual mobile games. We are trying to change that. We make sure to add a lot of customization options so players can make the game their own.

About Us

Telling Our Story Through Our Games

We are a Canadian indie game studio that started making mobile games in 2015 and are dedicated to making the best casual mobile games on the App Store and Google Play. We are trying to change the casual mobile gaming space by creating fully fledged games with rich content and an unmatched gameplay experience.
